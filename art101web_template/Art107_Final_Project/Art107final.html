ART 107 Final Project


For this project I had originally planned on making a game where you have a navigation app that does not lead you to where you want to go. Now, with our current pandemic I thought it might be interesting to do a Choose Your Own Adventure style game with the theme being ongoing events. I began by researching how grocery stores are operating during these times as well as how deliveries are being made.

Title: Sun Dial

Medium: Processing

Dimensions:500x500 pixels

This project utilizes circles expanding at rates corresponding to seconds, minutes, and hours. The circles expand until they hit the end of their cycle. With mouse pressed you can view tick marks to tell the time easier and you can press a letter key in order to view the actual time. The sun rotates around and the background changes color to represent the time of day.

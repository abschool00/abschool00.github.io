Andrew Barrios Project 1


Title: Father time and mother nature

Medium: Photoshop

Dimensions: 1200x1600 Pixels

Description: This composite takes the classic and well recognized piece of Grant Wood entitled American Gothic and uses the symbolism of a pocket watch and a potted plant in order to represent the imaginary forces of mother nature and father time. American Gothic is often used and modified to represent and showcase many different things. From political statements to artistic interpretations this piece is used to represent many different things to the viewer, in the case of this piece it is meant to give a physical and somewhat satirical embodyment to the forces which many people relate. This image lives purely online on this discussion feed in order to be changed and modified as time and nature often are.

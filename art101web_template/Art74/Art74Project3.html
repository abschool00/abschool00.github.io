Andrew Barrios Project 1


Title: Artificial nature

Medium: Audacity and Premiere Pro

Duration: 3:12

Description: I created this piece with the theme of artificial nature in mind. That meant recording things that were associated with nature but were not in fact natural. Once I found those sound clips I put them to the cadence of a watch ticking to show the passage of time. I placed the files in Audacity and faded the sounds in and out always going back to the ticking of the watch. I then exported the file into premiere to overlay the sound file with a picture of a calm river.
